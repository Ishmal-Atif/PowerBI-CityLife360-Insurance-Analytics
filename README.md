# PowerBI-CityLife360-Insurance-Analytics
Insurance and Finance Analytics

## Project Overview

City Life 360 is a multi-page Power BI dashboard designed for insurance and wealth management performance analysis.

The solution provides executives and business managers with insights into sales performance, withdrawals, advisor productivity, insurance products, regional trends, and wealth management activities.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query

## Business Areas Covered

### Executive Dashboard
- Net sales monitoring
- Sales performance tracking
- Withdrawal analysis
- Product performance overview
- Regional business analysis

### Wealth Management Analytics
- Mutual fund performance
- Retirement savings analysis
- Stock portfolio monitoring
- Advisor productivity evaluation
- Regional wealth distribution

### Insurance Analytics
- Car insurance analysis
- Home insurance analysis
- Life insurance analysis
- Policy sales tracking
- Advisor performance reporting

## Dashboard Features

- Interactive KPI Cards
- Year-over-Year Comparisons
- Product Performance Analysis
- Geographic Reporting
- Advisor Performance Tracking
- Risk Level Analysis
- Sales Trend Monitoring

## Key Insights

- Life insurance products generated the highest net sales.
- Advisor performance varied significantly across business lines.
- Wealth management products showed strong growth in retirement savings.
- Regional sales distribution revealed opportunities for targeted expansion.
- Multiple insurance categories experienced different year-over-year trends.
